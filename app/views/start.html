Welcome to Weathertop

This is the companion app for the WeatherTop consumer weather station kits. This app allows you to:

  • Access your collection of WeatherTop stations
  • Quickly view the latest readings for each station
  • See weather trends at a glance
  • Add or remove stations in your collection
  • Add or remove readings from your stations
  • Edit your profile details
